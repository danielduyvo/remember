Welcome to Remember
A small app for smart flashcards.

Inefficient Memorization

Rereading, cramming and listening to lectures are all techniques that are poor for retaining information.

The Forgetting Curve

Memory exponentially decays if not actively accessed or practiced. Retrieving memories reinforces the pathways they live in.

Spaced Repetition

The best way to remember something is to space out practice for it at increasing intervals.
